Yes, and these will be resized and printed on the A sized paper of your choice, with white borders above/below or left/right. You will be able to see what the finished document will look like in the previewer after uploading.

What are the hole-punching options for Wiro Binding?

For A5 and A4 ring binders, you can upload a portrait or landscape document and choose to bind it on the long or short edge. This gives four options; portrait document bound at the left (long edge - pages turn from the right) or bound at the top (short edge - pages turn from the bottom); landscape document bound at the left (short edge - pages turn from the right) or bound at the top (long edge - pages turn from the bottom).

What Hard and Soft binding?

Hard binding is a good quality rigid binding usually required for the final submission of your thesis.Pages are securely bound in for durability and have a rigid cover. They are covered in a library buckram or magesta book cloth which comes in a range of colours to suite any university specification.Our hard bindings are also lettered on the spine and/or the front cover in gold or silver foil. 

Soft binding is glue binding generally required for the first submission of your thesis. Soft bindings are cut flush all round and have enough rigidity to stand on a book shelf. Pages are secured with thermal glue for flexibility and strength and then covered in a library buckram book cloth which comes in a range of colours to suit any university requirement. Our soft binding includes gold or silver lettering to the spine.

For more informatios please visit our Binding Option page.


What is edge-to-edge printing?

Printing to the edge of the page eh? We get asked about this a lot. If you mean you would like your documents to have a colour or image going right up to the edge of the page with no ugly and annoying white margin then the simple answer is yes, of course!
In order to achieve this, when providing us your document we need it to have what are called ‘bleed‘ and ‘crop marks‘.
